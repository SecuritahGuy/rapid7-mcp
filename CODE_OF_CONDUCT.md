# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume positive intent
- Give and receive feedback professionally
- Focus on ideas, not individuals

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that violate these standards.

## Reporting

To report behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, contact:

- `maintainers@securitah.io`

All reports will be reviewed and handled as promptly and fairly as possible.
